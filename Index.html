About Sentiment Data Methodology

Overall Sentiment Score is provided directly from Marqii CSV exports — it is not calculated here. Marqii aggregates scores across Google, UberEats, DoorDash, and other connected review platforms using their weighted sentiment model (scored 0–5).
"vs prior" compares the current period to the immediately preceding period as defined in the Marqii data export. Upload updated Marqii CSVs each week via the Upload button to refresh.
Response Rate excludes stores with zero reviews in the current period. Ranking weights stores by total review volume — a 4.2 with 20 reviews ranks lower than a 4.2 with 2 reviews.
